Maharana Pratap(True Hero)

Maharana Pratap is best known for his resistance against the Mughal Empire, particularly Emperor and is considered a symbol of Rajput valor and is remembered for his bravery as well as for his unyielding spirit in defending his kingdom.His greatest battle was fought against the Mughals in the Battle of Haldighati in 1576.
